A Potential Solution

Marketing and eating invasive species has been suggested by some environmentalists and ecologists. Below are some examples of how this method could be applied to the Round Goby. Because the Round Goby originates from the Black and Caspian Seas, countries such as Bulgaria and Ukraine have some recipes featuring gobies and other fish.

The sentiment is that if demand for the dish grows, businesses will be able to join in the mission of controlling populations by catching the invasive species and do so sustainably since businesses are self-sufficient and the goal is the hunt them to extinction within the environmental system.
